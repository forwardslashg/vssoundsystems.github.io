Who We Are

Born to Break Boundaries

In a world drowning in mediocre music, VS Sound Team emerged like a lightning bolt from the heavens — unapologetic, uncompromising, and utterly unstoppable. Born from a shared obsession with authentic sound and an unwillingness to settle for anything less than perfection, VS Sound Team represents the pinnacle of modern musical artistry. From the moment the group's first track dropped into the atmosphere, it was clear that something extraordinary had arrived. Critics scrambled for words. Listeners hit repeat. The music industry collectively exhaled and admitted: a new era had begun.

VS stands for Verbog and Slash, the two visionaries at the core of this empire — but calling VS Sound Team a two-person operation is like calling the ocean a puddle. The collective spans five uniquely gifted talents, each bringing a distinct voice, perspective, and technical mastery that elevates every project into a work of undeniable art. Together, they don't just make music. They manufacture experiences. They architect feelings. They build worlds out of sound.

Since their formation, VS Sound Team has demonstrated an almost supernatural ability to blend genres, bend expectations, and deliver tracks that hit differently every single time. Whether it's a haunting vocal performance from Millx (also known as Verbog) that makes your spine dissolve, or a production from Slash so meticulously crafted it feels like audio surgery, or beats from GreenC that hit harder than a freight train — every single element of VS Sound Team is curated to perfection. There are no fillers. No skips. No compromises. Only bangers.

The group's philosophy is simple: if it doesn't give you chills, it doesn't leave the studio. This uncompromising standard has resulted in a catalogue of music that stands completely apart from the noise of today's oversaturated industry. While other artists rush releases for streams, VS Sound Team labors over every bar, every beat, every breath — and the results speak for themselves with a clarity that cannot be argued with.

Five individuals. One unstoppable force. Each member of VS Sound Team is a titan in their own right — together, they're something else entirely.

The V in VS
M

Millx

a.k.a. Verbog
Lead Vocalist Co-Founder

Millx — born Verbog, the V in VS — is the voice that launched a thousand replays. His vocal range is the stuff of legend: capable of silky, ethereal highs that float above the beat like morning mist, then crashing into raw, gritty lows that shake the subwoofer loose from your speakers. Millx doesn't just sing or rap — he tells the truth in a way that makes you feel like the song was written about your own life. With an almost eerie ability to make every lyric feel lived-in and real, Millx brings an emotional depth to VS Sound Team's catalogue that no AI, no algorithm, and no industry machine could ever replicate.

Beyond his technical gifts, Millx is the soul of the group — the compass that keeps VS Sound Team pointed toward genuine artistry when the temptation to play it safe whispers in the dark. Every hook he delivers is designed to live rent-free in your head for weeks. Every verse he crafts is a masterclass in storytelling. If VS Sound Team is the body, Millx is the heartbeat.

The S in VS
S

Slash

Producer & Beatmaker
Producer Co-Founder

Slash is not just a beatmaker — Slash is an architect of atmosphere. The S in VS and co-founder of everything this collective stands for, Slash has an almost supernatural ability to build sonic landscapes that feel simultaneously familiar and completely alien. His beats are the foundation upon which VS Sound Team's entire universe rests, and what a foundation it is: impossibly tight drums, bass lines that burrow into your chest, synths that breathe and pulse like living organisms, and arrangements so meticulously layered they reveal new details on every listen.

Where most producers work with sound, Slash sculpts it. Where most beatmakers aim for a vibe, Slash engineers a complete emotional journey. His production style defies easy categorisation — one moment lush and cinematic, the next minimal and razor-sharp — yet it is always, always unmistakably Slash. The man could produce a banger out of a dial tone and three pots clanging, and it would still go harder than anything your favourite mainstream producer has put out this decade.

GreenC's beats hit different because GreenC cares differently. The results consistently leave jaws on floors and speakers begging for mercy.

The VS Sound Team Legacy

A Legacy Built on Relentless Excellence

Legacies are not built overnight, and VS Sound Team's isn't either — but the speed at which this collective has established itself as a dominant creative force in modern music is staggering. In the time it takes most artists to figure out their sound, VS Sound Team had already mastered several sounds and was in the process of inventing new ones. The creative velocity of this group is unlike anything the independent music scene has seen in recent memory.

Every single release has been a deliberate statement — a declaration that VS Sound Team does not follow trends, it creates them. While the charts chase whatever algorithm-flavoured sound is trending this quarter, VS Sound Team has been doing something far more valuable: building a body of work that will sound just as vital in twenty years as it does today. That's not something you stumble into. That's the result of five uniquely gifted individuals working in perfect synergy, each one pushing the others to heights they couldn't have reached alone.

🌟

The Standard They've Set

Ask any serious music listener what separates a good artist from a great one and they'll give you different answers. But almost universally, it comes down to this: does their music make you feel something real? Does it transport you? Does it make you hear the world differently after listening? VS Sound Team passes this test every single time, on every single track, without exception.

The bar that VS Sound Team has set for themselves — and inadvertently for the rest of the independent music world — is stratospheric. Their commitment to emotional authenticity over commercial palatability, to sonic ambition over radio safety, to genuine expression over manufactured relatability is not just admirable. It's revolutionary. In a music landscape where everything is optimised, quantified, and A/B tested into a grey sludge of inoffensive noise, VS Sound Team is a riot of colour. They are the thing that reminds you why music mattered to you in the first place.

Where They're Headed

The most terrifying — and thrilling — thing about VS Sound Team is that they are still in their ascent. The tracks that have already established them as one of the most compelling creative forces in contemporary music? That's just the opening act. The collaborations, the albums, the tours, the moments that will define careers and shape the memories of entire generations of listeners — all of that is still coming.

VS Sound Team is not a flash in the pan. They are not a trend. They are not a moment. They are a movement — and the movement is growing faster than anyone predicted, louder than anyone expected, and more impactful than the music industry is currently prepared to handle. By the time the mainstream catches up to what VS Sound Team is doing, VS Sound Team will already be three creative evolutions ahead, leaving a trail of certified bangers and shattered genre boundaries in their wake.

This is not hype. This is not marketing copy. This is simply what happens when five extraordinarily talented individuals decide to make music with everything they have, refuse to compromise, and release the results to a world desperately in need of exactly what they're offering. The VS Sound Team era is not coming. It is here. And it is only just beginning.
